Equine Park

Equine Park is surrounded by various known amenities and facilities. There is a Mcdonald’s Drive Through and AEON Jusco is just 2 minutes away from Equine Park. Grocery shopping are made easy too with many shopping malls such as Giant Hypermarket, The Atmosphere, Pasar Borong Selangor, Putra Walk, IOI Mall Puchong, IOI Resort City Putrajaya and Sierra Zentro only 15 minutes away from Taman Equine. Moreover, 50 metres away from Equine Park will lead you to SKVE and LDP. Public transportations such as buses and taxis are easily accessible as well.

In addition, schools are easily access from Equine Park as there are a number of international schools and universities in the vicinity of Equine Park, such as Universiti Putra Malaysia, Multimedia University, Lim Kok Wing University of Creative Technology, John Hopkins University, Segi University, Alice Smith International School, Rafflesia International and Private Schools, ELC International School, Tanarata International School Kajang, Australia International School and Taylor’s International School, Puchong.
